What is Lina Labs? 

Lina is an accessible and low-bandwidth support and management resource for underserved Filipino learners. 


What is an LMS (learning management system)?

The ongoing pandemic emphasizes getting work done online in order to minimize transmission. A learning management system is a software application that strives to plan, implement, or assess learning processes. At Lina, we offer learning opportunities and resources such as worksheets, school content, and interactive features between the pupil and teacher. By doing so, we're able to fill in the learning gaps for underprivileged Filipino students, allowing them to graduate and achieve future endeavors. 


Is Lina a non-profit organization?

Yes! Lina is a student-run organization seeking to help our target community without having to seek profit. We strive to be accessible as possible in providing education for these students.


Who does Lina Labs work with?

Lina Labs has partnered with organizations such as the Philippine High School History Movement, and Habi Education Lab.
