Is Lina available on both iOS and Android? 

At the moment, Lina is on Android but will expand to iOS very soon. Stay tuned! 


How do I create an account in the app?

Step 1: Download the Lina app. 


Step 2: Once you log into the Lina app, you will need your phone number to create an account. The app will then send you an OTP to verify your phone number.


Step 3: After verifying your phone number, you will be asked to create a password.


Step 4: When you’ve created a secure password, you will then be asked to log in the necessary information like your name, school, etc. When you’ve completed your information, your teacher will give you a code to enter your virtual classrooms.


Step 5: You're free to use Lina to assist you in your studies!


Do you offer offline resources? 

We are currently trying to work out an offline option for the app, without compromising the quality of our content. Nevertheless, we are managing to provide opportunities and support for all conditions of students. 


Do you create all of the resources? 

Yes. Lina has extremely dedicated students and teachers tasked with specific subject groups to ensure that each topic is well-explained for students to fully understand.


Will Lina Labs be available in other countries?

Yes. As of now, we are available in the Phillippines but as we expand, we plan to expand around the Southeast Asian region. This includes providing content with translations for each different language across countries.


How do I apply to work with Lina Labs? 

Go to the “Apply Now!” page. You will be directed to a link to our application form in which you would be required to fill out all fields. Once done, your application will be assessed until you will be scheduled for an interview with one of our admin members. If your application has been approved, you will be put through a series of onboarding tasks for our team to assess your skillset.
