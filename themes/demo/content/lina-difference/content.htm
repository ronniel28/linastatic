Bakit Lina?

We’re run by some of the world’s most passionate high-school students and teachers, straight from all around the Philippines. Who else would be better suited to run a project like Lina?

 

Our library of curriculum-aligned resources are highly accessible, no matter who you are, where you are, or what you are! All our Lina products are functional under low-bandwidth conditions and downloadable. All you need is to show up, open your phone, and access our Lina notes! 

 

Lina goes beyond content. We believe in the power of community, and prioritize the creation of a safe space for the Filipino youth. Our students support, teach, and learn from one another. We consider our students family. 

 

We are the biggest educational community, valuing crowdsourced and student-made resources. We believe in the power of the earth-shaking power of the youth, and take every opportunity to highlight their voices throughout our ecosystem.

 

We are always expanding our reach. We believe in the importance of lifelong learning, and are constantly building new courses and programs that extend way beyond what’s provided by local standards.

A place...

Tubbataha Reef

Bright, colorful, and vibrant, serving as a home for millions of

 indigenous animals, and popularly termed “The Crown Jewel of the

 Philippines”, Tubbataha Reef stands for the diversity of life in the

 Philippines. Within the reef system exist thousands of self-sustaining

 and symbiotic communities, paralleling the student-led siblinghood

 built amongst Lina Tarlings.

A dish...

Pancit Canton

Simple to make, but requiring many ingredients, Pancit

 Canton is the epitome of a family meal in Filipino

 culture. Drawing from cultures all over the Philippines,

 the dish ultimately comes together through vigorous 

tossing and cooking, creating a beautiful combination

 of flavors and textures. Eating Pancit Canton brings a

 familiar sense of comfort whenever eaten.
